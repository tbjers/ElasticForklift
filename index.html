Welcome to ElasticForklift

ElasticForklift works like Elastic Beanstalk in the sense that it allows you to publish your project to a load balanced high availability cluster in the AWS cloud. Written mostly in PHP it allows you to deploy any type of product into your cluster by letting you define your own instance images and lets you fine tune how your Load Balancer operates.
